Course 01
Concealed Carry
(CCW)
Duration
8 Hours
Format
Classroom + Live Fire
Prereqs
None — all levels

Colorado's concealed carry permit requires demonstrated competency with a handgun and knowledge of state law. This full-day course covers everything you need to carry legally and confidently.

What You'll Cover
  • Colorado concealed carry law and legal use of force
  • Safe storage, transportation, and handling
  • Situational awareness and threat recognition
  • Drawing, presenting, and re-holstering safely
  • Live-fire qualification shoot
  • Permit application walkthrough
What to bring: Valid Colorado ID, handgun in good working order, 50 rounds of ammunition, eye and ear protection (loaners available).

Course 03
Defensive Shooting
Tactics
Duration
6 Hours
Format
Range-Based
Prereqs
Pistol Fundamentals

This is where training gets real. Designed for students who already know the basics, Defensive Shooting Tactics introduces the skills and mindset needed to protect yourself when it counts.

What You'll Cover
  • Drawing from a holster under time pressure
  • Shooting on the move — lateral, forward, rearward
  • Low-light and reduced-visibility drills
  • Multiple-target engagement and priority decisions
  • Cover vs. concealment — real-world application
  • Mindset and decision-making under stress
What to bring: Handgun, OWB or IWB holster, 150 rounds of ammunition, eye and ear protection.

Course 06
Range Safety
Officer
Duration
8 Hours
Format
NRA Certified
Prereqs
Basic safety knowledge

Earn your NRA Range Safety Officer certification and be qualified to run safe, efficient ranges at clubs, competitions, or public facilities. This course is also ideal for firearm instructors looking to expand their credentials.

What You'll Cover
  • NRA range standard operating procedures
  • Supervising and commanding shooters on the line
  • Hazard identification and risk mitigation
  • Emergency response — ceasefire, injuries, misfires
  • Handling prohibited or unsafe firearms
  • Written exam + practical range evaluation
Certification: Graduates receive NRA RSO credentials valid nationwide. Certificate issued same day upon passing.
